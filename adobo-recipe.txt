Adobo Recipe

2 Tablespoons vegetabel oil
3 pound chicken or pork cut into pieces
1 large red onion, quartered and sliced
1/2 cup vinegar 
1/2 cup soy sauce
3 tabelspoons of minced garlic
1 tablespoon of black pepper
3 bay leaf
2 potatoes (diced)

1) Heat oil on large skillet, cook/fry chicken/pork until golden brown on both/all sides.
2) Temporarily remove meat.
3) Stir in onion and garlic until soft and light brown.
4) Pour vinegar, soy sauce, black peper, bay leaf, browned chicken
5) Bring to boil
6) Simmer and cover for 30 minutes until meat is cooked
